TCA PEELING 15

Trichloroacetic acid acts by denaturing the proteins of the epidermis, causing ischemic necrosis, evidenced by the formation of frosting (whitening) and alterations in the elasticity of the skin. Indications:
  • Moderate photoageing.
  • Moderate post-acne marks.
