Hair cocktail plus

  • Description
  • Active ingredients

TOSKANI Hair Cocktail Plus formula combines different types of ingredients that act at different levels and phases of the hair growth cycle. Hair Cocktail Plus establishes cellular regeneration systems, prevents hormonal imbalance and common glandular problems in male and female hair loss situations. This mesotherapy product induces vasodilation in order to achieve an adequate perfusion to the cells of the capillary bulb, optimising the supply of nutrients. The content with high concentrations of vitamins of group B guarantees a complete nutrition of the dermal papillae, as well as a correct metabolic functioning. Thanks to its content of Sabal serrulata, Hair Cocktail Plus has a great effectiveness in hair loss treatments for men.

Indications:
  • Alopecia
  • Low skin trophism
  • Hair loss

HCPR

  • Description
  • Active ingredients

TOSKANI HCPR is a peptide-based mesotherapy cocktail to prevent hair loss and alopecia in women and men. This treatment induces hair growth by revitalising the hair follicles and stimulating the blood microcirculation. HCPR improves problems in the scalp by hydrating and nourishing it. This TOSKANI cocktail also strenghtens the hair root and increases the thickness and density of hair.

Indications:
  • Male-pattern baldness
  • Female-pattern baldness
  • Alopecia areata
  • Scarring alopecia
